Scratch

This project was created for Harvard's CSCI E7 Intro to Python course to demonstrate the use of scripts with conditions, loops and variables. It also uses blocks, sounds and parameters.

Its a breaking news segment that uses humor to tell a story about humans landing on Neptune and the sending back the selfies the astronauts took.
